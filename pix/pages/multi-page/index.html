About me

The Girl Behind the Lens.

I am seventeen years old and have been experimenting with photography for around three years. I mainly shoot landscapes and animals but portraits are my absolute favorite things to do. I want to venture in to light and astrophotograpy in the fututre.

I plan on majoring in Environmental Studies and Policy in college but my dream is to become a nature photographer for National Geographic. My main insiprations are James Balog and Annie Leibovitz. Currently I am working on a year long photo-a-day series on Instagram that I started on the first of January.
